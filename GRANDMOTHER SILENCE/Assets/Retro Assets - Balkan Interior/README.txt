WELCOME TO PSX BALKAN PROJECT

ABOUT
DO YOU LIKE SHOPSKA SALAD!?. In this project i focused average balkan house life style. My target is creating the furniture,tools,items, architecture details and the aspect of life style of balkans from all balkan countries from 50s to 2000s. If i assume you don't know what is balkan it's called "eastern europe" as well. Balkan region includes Albania, Bosnia, Bulgaria, Croatia, Greece, Kosovo, North Macedonia, Montenegro, Romania, Slovenia, Serbia and a small part of Turkey (Thrace).

I hope every balkan related person and everyone around the world likes this project.

ART STYLE
Models are made from real life photos of the objects. Few of them are my families or from near villages furniture. I took photos with my phone for the textures. Modification of the texture is 32/16 Color limit, Dithered and downsized to 256 or 512 px.
